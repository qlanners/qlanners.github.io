## Neural Machine Translation [<font size="2">Repo</font>](https://github.com/qlanners/nmt_tutorial)
<img src="{{site.url}}/images/encoder.png" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />
<img src="{{site.url}}/images/decoder.png" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />
Worked under the guidance of [Dr. Thomas Laurent](http://thomaslaurent.lmu.build/homepage.html) of Loyola Marymount University's Mathematics Department to enhance the PyTorch tutorial ["NLP From Scratch: Translation with a sequence to sequence network and attention"](https://pytorch.org/tutorials/intermediate/seq2seq_translation_tutorial.html). Culminated in a research paper which can be found [here](https://github.com/qlanners/nmt_tutorial/blob/master/quinn_thesis_final.pdf) and a technical blog which can be found [here](https://towardsdatascience.com/neural-machine-translation-15ecf6b0b).

This work was presented first to LMU faculty and staff and later at [Optum's MinneAnalytics conference](http://minneanalytics.org/optum2019/).

## Analysis of Atmoshperic Gas Concentration Samples [<font size="2">Repo</font>](https://github.com/qlanners/atmospheric_concentration_analysis)
<img src="{{site.url}}/images/SCURR_2017_Poster.png" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />
Assisted [Dr. Lambert Doezema](https://cse.lmu.edu/department/envs/faculty/?expert=lambert.doezema) of Loyola Marymount University's Chemistry Department with data analysis. Specifically, used python to plot, map, and analyze various atmospheric gas concentrations collected over a ten year period by [NASA's Student Airborne Research Program](https://airbornescience.nasa.gov/nsrc/sarp).

This work was presented at the 2017 Southern California Conferences for Undergraduate Research.
